1: Sign up

Use our easy sign up form to add your name to the J&R bookclub list. No email? No problem, call the phone number at the top of the page and we will enter your details for you

2: Find your person

We pair you with a person of a different generation who we think you will relate with

3: Get reading

Pick your first book and both get reading. Agree on a time and date to chat through the book so far. We suggest every chapter or couple of chapters, but it's upto you!

4: Get sociable!

Call or answer the phone at the agreed time and enjoy the conversation.
